Our Story

We are a community of makers, dreamers, and fiber artists dedicated to keeping the craft alive.

More Than Just Yarn

It started with a simple idea: why create alone when you can create together? Founded in 2023, Needle & Thread has grown from a small local group to a global platform connecting thousands of knitters and crocheters.

We believe that handmade items carry a piece of the maker's soul. Our mission is to provide the resources, inspiration, and community support needed for everyone to express themselves through fiber arts.
