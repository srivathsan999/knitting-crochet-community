Textured Infinity Scarf

by Mike Wool

Free

A beautiful infinity scarf featuring rich textured stitches that create visual interest and warmth. This beginner-friendly pattern is perfect for learning new stitch techniques.

Skill Level Beginner
Estimated Time 3 Days
Yarn Weight Bulky (5)
Needle Size US 10 / 6mm
Gauge 14 sts x 20 rows
Language English

Description

This free infinity scarf pattern features a combination of knit and purl stitches to create beautiful texture. The pattern is worked in the round, making it seamless and reversible.

Perfect for beginners looking to expand their skills beyond basic knitting. Includes detailed instructions for joining in the round and maintaining even tension. The finished scarf is approximately 60" in circumference and 10" wide.
